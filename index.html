About me

I am a researcher at the Department of Mathematics of Ghent University. My research is primarily on mathematical logic, especially modal logic and proof theory. I am also interested in epistemic logic and cryptography.

I hold a bachelor's degree in Applied Mathematics from ITAM and a PhD from Stanford University. My doctoral advisor was Grigori Mints.
